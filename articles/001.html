Article 001 · Editorial

AgentDub.ai is an independent editorial platform dedicated to the rise of AI agents, autonomous systems, and applied artificial intelligence.

As artificial intelligence moves beyond interaction and into execution, a new class of systems is emerging — systems that can plan, act, coordinate, and operate autonomously within defined boundaries.

These AI agents are not a distant concept. They are already reshaping how organizations build software, automate workflows, and make decisions.

AgentDub.ai exists to document this transition clearly and independently, with a specific focus on execution, infrastructure, and governance rather than speculation or hype.

With Dubai positioning itself as a global hub for applied AI and digital transformation, the region provides a unique vantage point to observe how autonomous systems move from experimentation into real-world deployment.

This publication is written for founders, investors, executives, and institutions who want to understand not just what AI agents can do — but how they can be deployed responsibly and at scale.
