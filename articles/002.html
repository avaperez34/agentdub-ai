AI Agents: From Interaction to Execution

Traditional AI systems respond. AI agents act.

The shift from conversational interfaces to autonomous execution marks a fundamental change in how software creates value.

Agents can plan tasks, call tools, coordinate systems, and operate within defined constraints — turning AI from an assistant into an operator.

This evolution introduces both opportunity and responsibility. Execution at scale requires trust, visibility, and control.
